Hi, welcome to my personal website!
I am a Ph.D. in Finance at Southwestern University of Finance and Economics, with a primary focus on asset pricing.

I graduate in June 2024, and have overseas academic experiences in research institutions such as Washington University in St. Louis, Columbia University, and University of California.

My research mainly focuses on empirical asset pricing and corporate bond pricing, trying to understand the pattern of securities return movements:

  • What are the factors that affecting securities prices?

  • How expected returns vary over business cycles and across different asset classes?

  • Can we derive predictability of stock and bond returns through historical observations?

Feel free to contact me for collaboration and research discussions!
